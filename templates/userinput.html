Spotify Match

This app compares two spotify users and produces a percentage difference in their music tastes by calculating the euclidean distance of "music profiles" from each other. These music profiles are generated using data from their saved spotify playlists. Enter two profile share links to begin. If you know both usernames, you can input each as "spotify/(USERNAME HERE)?user".
